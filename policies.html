We use Firebase by Google to store and manage data. Firebase may collect certain information to improve our app's functionality. The types of data collected include:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. Data is stored securely within Firebase, which uses robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access.
